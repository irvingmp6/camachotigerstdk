Master Carlos Camacho

Master Camacho is a distinguished Tae Kwon Do instructor with over 25 years of experience in both training and teaching martial arts. He began his journey at the age of 3 and became a certified instructor by the age of 15. Since then, he has dedicated himself to guiding students of all ages and backgrounds toward achieving their black belts.

Philosophy

Master Camacho believes in the holistic development of his students, focusing on both physical fitness and mental discipline. His training methods incorporate traditional Tae Kwon Do techniques with modern fitness principles.

Achievements

  • 5th-degree black belt in Tae Kwon Do
  • Certified Tae Kwon Do instructor
  • Multiple national and international championships
  • Mentor to numerous black belts
